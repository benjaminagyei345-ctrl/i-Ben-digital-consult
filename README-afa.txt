
Instructions to Add AFA Products to Your Site
--------------------------------------------
1. Open your GitHub repo and go to index.html.
2. Locate the Services section (search for id="services").
3. Paste the HTML from 'services-afa.html' where you want the AFA products to appear.
4. Upload 'afa.js' to your site and include it at the end of your HTML before </body>:
   <script src="afa.js"></script>
5. Replace YOURNUMBER in afa.js with your actual WhatsApp business number (format: 233XXXXXXXXX).
6. Commit and deploy.
